VISION RING

The Vision Ring is an innovative twist to a problem for the blind

PROBLEM

New technologies have come into our lives that have made reading braille not enough for the visually impaired to survive. Those who gain blindness older will have trouble adjusting due to their memory systems.

HOW IT WORKS

Inside the ring is a tiny bluetooth camera that will sense your motions once your finger is pointed out. Once the finger is pointed, the scanning process will begin using AI technology. Once the scan has been identified, the information will be relayed to the head set in the ear.

PURPOSE

The Vision Ring works as a new way for the blind to deal with their disability with the easiest way possible. It will be almost like having a superpower.
