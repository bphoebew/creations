SWEET DREAMS

PURPOSE

Everything is dark in the room, drawing attention to the light objects, which are the common technologies. The girl is holding a picture of the stuffed bear that used to be, but in reality it is torn and broken. This is meant to represent the disconnect children are starting to face with everyday objects. As technology further improves in our lives, children are exposed at younger ages. This is making stuffed animals and other toys increasingly obsolete.
